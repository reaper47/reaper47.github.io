Hoppy Beaver Extreme

The goal of this project was to create a game where you avoid and collect objects and collect. Select your hero, click on play, and rule the world using arrow keys! Note: Refresh the page to load the hero images if they do not appear. Also, before pressing 'play', click on the canvas, and press a key to focus on it. To play the game once again, refesh the page.
