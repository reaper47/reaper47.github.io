Travel to Quebec City, Canada

As seen above, Quebec City during winter is beautiful. The river the boat is travelling on is the St. Lawrence River, which connects the Great Lakes in Ontario with the Atlantic Ocean. Watch as the frozen winds and seas engulf the city in a vortex of majestic sceneries, great nightlife, polite citizens, and an enjoyable general atmosphere.

  • Take a day off to explore the Montmorency Falls Park
  • Admire the view of Quebec's set of two bridges
